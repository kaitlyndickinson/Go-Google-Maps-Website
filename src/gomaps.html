Go Maps

Enter a location in the form: Address, City, State Zipcode

Example: 3201 Campus Dr, Klamath Falls, OR 97601

You can also enter the name of a place, such as Brevada Brewhouse and Google Maps will usually figure it out

Zoom level from 0-30, 0 will show the entire world map and 20-30 will show a close up view of the location

Map size needs to be in the form WidthxHeight

Map type can be roadmap, satellite, hybrid, or terrain

For best results enter all fields or only supply a geographic location

Press Generate Map when you are ready to generate your map!
